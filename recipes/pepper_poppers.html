Pepper Poppers

Home

Description

This recipe is a recreation of the dish from the videogame, Stardew Valley. It is spicy breaded peppers filled with cheese. In game, it is loved by the characters Maru and Shane, and gives a temporary boost to farming and speed when eaten.

Ingredients

  • 1 dozen jalapenos
  • 175 grams cream cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozarella cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 1/2 cups panko breadcrumbs
  • Spices: paprika, salt, pepper, cayenne, garlic powder, onion powder, oregano (to taste)

Steps

  1. Rinse the jalapenos and slice off the tops. Remove the membranes and seeds inside.
  2. Microwave the cream cheese for 20 seconds to soften, then place it in a bowl with the shredded mozarella cheese. Add 1 teaspoon of paprika and 1/2 teaspoon of cayenne, mixing together until creamy.
  3. In a bowl, beat the two eggs and add in the milk. Mix in 1 teaspoon of: salt, pepper, oregano, garlic powder, and onion powder.
  4. Set out two plates/bowls. In one, mix together the breadcrumbs, 2 teaspoons oregano, and 1 teaspoon of salt and pepper, respectively. In the other, mix together the flour and 1 teaspoon paprika.
  5. Stuff the peppers with the cream cheese filling. Roll in the flour until well coated, then dunk in the egg mixture. Fully coat the peppers in the egg, then roll them in the panko breadcrumbs. Repeat if needed.
  6. Place the peppers on a lightly greased baking sheet and bake at 350 degrees F (178 degrees C) for ~35 minutes.
